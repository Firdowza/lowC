10-Day Low-Carb Recipes

Day 1

Breakfast: Scrambled Eggs with Avocado and Spinach
  • Ingredients: 2 eggs, 1/2 avocado, 1 cup spinach, 1 tsp olive oil
  • Instructions: Heat olive oil, sauté spinach, scramble eggs, serve with avocado.
Lunch: Grilled Chicken Salad
  • Ingredients: 1 grilled chicken breast, 2 cups mixed greens, 1/2 cucumber, 1 small tomato, 1 tbsp olive oil & lemon juice
  • Instructions: Slice chicken, mix with salad ingredients, drizzle with dressing.
Dinner: Baked Salmon with Asparagus
  • Ingredients: 1 salmon fillet, 8-10 asparagus, 1 tbsp olive oil, garlic, lemon juice
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 400°F, season and bake salmon and asparagus for 15-20 mins.

Day 2

Breakfast: Greek Yogurt with Chia Seeds and Berries
  • Ingredients: 1/2 cup Greek yogurt, 1 tsp chia seeds, 1/4 cup berries
  • Instructions: Mix chia seeds into yogurt, top with berries.
Lunch: Turkey Lettuce Wraps
  • Ingredients: 4 large lettuce leaves, 4 slices deli turkey, 1/2 avocado, 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • Instructions: Place turkey, avocado, and carrots in lettuce wraps and roll.
Dinner: Cauliflower Stir-Fry with Shrimp
  • Ingredients: 1 cup cauliflower rice, 1/2 lb shrimp, 1/2 red bell pepper, 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Instructions: Sauté shrimp, add cauliflower rice and veggies, stir in soy sauce.

Day 3

Breakfast: Veggie Omelette
  • Ingredients: 2 eggs, 1/4 cup diced mushrooms, 1/4 cup spinach, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Instructions: Sauté veggies, whisk eggs, pour over veggies, cook until set.
Lunch: Tuna Salad Lettuce Wraps
  • Ingredients: 1 can tuna, 2 tbsp mayo, 1/4 avocado, 4 large lettuce leaves
  • Instructions: Mix tuna with mayo and avocado, serve in lettuce wraps.
Dinner: Grilled Steak with Roasted Brussels Sprouts
  • Ingredients: 1 steak, 1 cup Brussels sprouts, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Instructions: Grill steak, roast Brussels sprouts at 400°F for 25 mins.

Day 4

Breakfast: Chia Pudding with Almonds
  • Ingredients: 2 tbsp chia seeds, 1/2 cup almond milk, 1/4 cup crushed almonds
  • Instructions: Mix chia seeds with almond milk, let sit for 1-2 hours, top with almonds.
Lunch: Cobb Salad
  • Ingredients: 2 cups mixed greens, 1 boiled egg, 1/2 avocado, 1/2 cup chicken, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Instructions: Toss all ingredients together with dressing.
Dinner: Zucchini Noodles with Pesto and Chicken
  • Ingredients: 1 zucchini, 1 chicken breast, 2 tbsp pesto
  • Instructions: Sauté zucchini noodles, toss with pesto and chicken.

Day 5

Breakfast: Smoked Salmon and Eggs
  • Ingredients: 2 eggs, 2 oz smoked salmon, 1 tbsp cream cheese
  • Instructions: Scramble eggs, serve with salmon and cream cheese.
Lunch: Grilled Chicken and Veggies
  • Ingredients: 1 grilled chicken breast, 1/2 cup broccoli, 1/2 cup roasted bell pepper
  • Instructions: Serve chicken with broccoli and roasted peppers.
Dinner: Lamb Chops with Cauliflower Mash
  • Ingredients: 2 lamb chops, 1 cup cauliflower, 1 tbsp butter
  • Instructions: Grill lamb, mash steamed cauliflower with butter.

Day 6

Breakfast: Coconut Flour Pancakes
  • Ingredients: 2 tbsp coconut flour, 2 eggs, 1/4 cup almond milk, 1 tsp baking powder
  • Instructions: Mix ingredients, cook pancakes on a griddle.
Lunch: Shrimp and Avocado Salad
  • Ingredients: 1/2 lb shrimp, 1/2 avocado, 2 cups mixed greens, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Instructions: Mix shrimp, avocado, and greens, drizzle with olive oil.
Dinner: Baked Chicken Thighs with Green Beans
  • Ingredients: 2 chicken thighs, 1 cup
  • Ingredients: 2 chicken thighs, 1 cup green beans, 1 tbsp olive oil, garlic powder
  • Instructions: Preheat oven to 400°F, season chicken, bake for 30 mins, add green beans for the last 10 mins.

Day 7

Breakfast: Egg Muffins
  • Ingredients: 6 eggs, 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers, 1/2 cup spinach, 1/4 cup cheese
  • Instructions: Whisk eggs, add veggies and cheese, pour into muffin tins, bake at 350°F for 20 mins.
Lunch: Salmon and Cucumber Salad
  • Ingredients: 1 can salmon, 1/2 cucumber, 1 tbsp mayo, 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Instructions: Mix salmon with cucumber, mayo, and lemon juice.
Dinner: Grilled Pork Chops with Roasted Cauliflower
  • Ingredients: 2 pork chops, 1 cup cauliflower, 1 tbsp olive oil, rosemary
  • Instructions: Season pork chops, grill, roast cauliflower at 400°F for 20 mins.

Day 8

Breakfast: Cottage Cheese with Berries
  • Ingredients: 1 cup cottage cheese, 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • Instructions: Combine cottage cheese with berries.
Lunch: Chicken Avocado Salad
  • Ingredients: 1 grilled chicken breast, 1/2 avocado, 2 cups spinach, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Instructions: Mix chicken, avocado, and spinach, drizzle with olive oil.
Dinner: Beef Stir-Fry with Zucchini
  • Ingredients: 1/2 lb beef strips, 1 zucchini, 1 bell pepper, soy sauce
  • Instructions: Sauté beef, add veggies and soy sauce, cook until veggies are tender.

Day 9

Breakfast: Avocado and Bacon Scramble
  • Ingredients: 2 eggs, 1/2 avocado, 2 slices cooked bacon
  • Instructions: Scramble eggs, mix in diced avocado and bacon.
Lunch: Turkey Spinach Roll-ups
  • Ingredients: 4 slices turkey, 1 cup spinach, 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • Instructions: Spread cream cheese on turkey, add spinach, roll up.
Dinner: Grilled Cod with Sautéed Spinach
  • Ingredients: 1 cod fillet, 2 cups spinach, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Instructions: Grill cod, sauté spinach in olive oil.

Day 10

Breakfast: Boiled Eggs and Avocado
  • Ingredients: 2 boiled eggs, 1/2 avocado, salt and pepper
  • Instructions: Slice avocado, serve with boiled eggs.
Lunch: Zucchini Noodles with Pesto and Chicken
  • Ingredients: 1 zucchini, 1 grilled chicken breast, 2 tbsp pesto
  • Instructions: Spiralize zucchini, sauté, toss with chicken and pesto.
Dinner: Stir-fried Tofu with Broccoli
  • Ingredients: 1 block tofu, 2 cups broccoli, 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Instructions: Sauté tofu until golden, add broccoli, stir in soy sauce.
